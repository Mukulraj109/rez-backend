## Inviting Team Members

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Navigate to Team Management
- Log in to your merchant dashboard
- Go to **Settings** â†’ **Team Management**

#### 2. Click "Invite Team Member"

#### 3. Fill in Details

## Security Best Practices

### âœ… Do's

#### 1. Choose Roles Carefully
- Give people the **minimum access** they need
- Staff for frontline â†’ Manager for supervisors â†’ Admin for trusted managers

#### 2. Review Team Regularly
- Monthly check: Who has access?
- Remove people who have left
- Adjust roles as responsibilities change

#### 3. Use Strong Passwords
- Require team members to use strong passwords
- Don't share passwords
- Change passwords if compromised

#### 4. Monitor Activity
- Check who's logging in
- Review activity logs
- Investigate suspicious behavior

#### 5. Suspend, Don't Delete (Initially)
- If unsure, suspend first
- Can reactivate if needed
- Delete only when certain

### âŒ Don'ts

#### 1. Don't Give Everyone Admin
- Only truly trusted people should be Admin
- Most people need Manager or Staff

#### 2. Don't Share Your Owner Account
- Keep owner credentials private
- Create separate accounts for team members
- Never let anyone log in as you

#### 3. Don't Ignore Suspended Accounts
- Review suspended accounts quarterly
- Remove if no longer needed
- Reactivate or delete

#### 4. Don't Leave Invitations Open
- Invitations expire in 24 hours
- Resend if needed
- Don't create "just in case" invitations

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Hiring a Store Manager

**Situation:** You hired Sarah to manage your store. She needs to handle products, orders, and customer service.

**Solution:**
1. Invite Sarah as **Admin**
2. She accepts and sets password
3. Sarah can now:
   - Add/edit/delete products
   - Process orders and refunds
   - Hire and manage staff
   - View sales analytics

**Why Admin?** Store manager needs broad access to run operations.

---

### Scenario 2: Part-Time Sales Associate

**Situation:** You hired Mike as a part-time sales associate. He needs to check inventory and update orders.

**Solution:**
1. Invite Mike as **Staff**
2. Mike accepts and sets password
3. Mike can now:
   - View product inventory
   - See order status
   - Update order status
   - View customer info

**Why Staff?** Sales associates don't need edit/delete access.

---

### Scenario 3: Seasonal Help

**Situation:** Holiday rush! You hired 3 temporary workers for 2 months.

**Solution:**
1. Invite all 3 as **Staff**
2. They help during busy season
3. After 2 months:
   - **Option A:** Suspend accounts (if they might return)
   - **Option B:** Remove accounts (if permanent)

**Why Staff?** Temporary workers need limited access.

---

### Scenario 4: Promoting an Employee

**Situation:** Your manager John has proven himself. You want to give him more responsibility.

**Solution:**
1. Go to Team Management
2. Find John (currently Manager)
3. Change role to **Admin**
4. John now has Admin access

**Result:** John can now manage team and access financial data.

---

### Scenario 5: Someone Left the Company

**Situation:** Your admin Lisa left the company last week.

**Solution:**
1. **Immediate:** Suspend Lisa's account
2. **Within a week:** Review what Lisa had access to
3. **Within a month:** Remove Lisa's account
4. **Check:** Activity logs for Lisa's final actions

**Why this order?** Suspend immediately for security, remove after confirming no issues.

---

### Scenario 6: Security Breach Concern

**Situation:** You think someone's account may be compromised.

**Solution:**
1. **Immediately:** Suspend the account
2. **Review:** Activity logs for unusual activity
3. **Contact:** The team member to confirm
4. **If compromised:**
   - Remove account
   - Create new invitation
   - They set new password
5. **If false alarm:**
   - Reactivate account
   - Recommend password change

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Invitation email not received

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Resend invitation
4. Try different email address

---

### Problem: Invitation link expired

**Solution:**
1. Go to Team Management
2. Find pending invitation
3. Click "Resend Invitation"
4. Team member has new 24-hour window

---

### Problem: Team member can't log in

**Check:**
1. Is account active or suspended?
2. Are they using correct email?
3. Did they set password after accepting invitation?
4. Is account locked after failed attempts?

**Solution:**
- If suspended: Reactivate account
- If locked: Wait 30 minutes or reset password
- If forgot password: Use password reset feature

---

### Problem: Team member needs different permissions

**Solution:**
1. If they need MORE access: Change their role (Owner only)
2. If they need LESS access: Change to lower role or remove specific abilities

---

### Problem: Can't remove a team member

**Possible reasons:**
1. You're trying to remove yourself (not allowed)
2. You're trying to remove the owner (not possible)
3. You don't have Admin/Owner access

**Solution:**
- Ask an Admin or Owner to remove them
- Or suspend the account instead

---

### Problem: Someone has too much access

**Solution:**
1. **Immediately:** Suspend their account
2. **Review:** What they've accessed
3. **Then:**
   - Change to appropriate role, OR
   - Remove account entirely
