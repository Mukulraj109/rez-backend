# Merchant Onboarding Guide

**Welcome to Your Store!**

This guide will help you complete your merchant onboarding in just 5 simple steps.

## Getting Started

After registering your merchant account, you'll be guided through a simple 5-step onboarding wizard. You can:

- **Save progress** at any time
- **Resume later** from where you left off
- **Edit information** before final submission
- **Track progress** with our visual progress bar

**Estimated Time:** 15-20 minutes

---

## Step 1: Business Information (Required)

### What You'll Need:
- ‚úÖ Company/Business Name
- ‚úÖ Business Type (Retail, Wholesale, Service, etc.)
- Registration Number (if registered)
- GST Number (if applicable)
- PAN Number (if applicable)

### Tips:
- Use your **official business name** as registered
- Select the business type that best describes your operations
- **GST Number Format:** 22AAAAA0000A1Z5 (15 characters)
- **PAN Number Format:** AAAAA9999A (10 characters)
- If you don't have GST/PAN, you can skip these fields

### Why We Need This:
We need to verify your business identity and ensure compliance with tax regulations.

## Step 2: Store Details (Required)

### What You'll Need:
- ‚úÖ Store Name (as it will appear to customers)
- ‚úÖ Store Category (Electronics, Fashion, Food, etc.)
- ‚úÖ Complete Address:
  - Street Address
  - City
  - State/Province
  - Postal/ZIP Code
  - Country
  - Landmark (optional)
- Store Description (optional but recommended)
- Store Logo (optional)
- Store Banner Image (optional)

### Tips:
- Choose a **memorable store name** that reflects your brand

- Add a compelling description (500 characters max)

### Image Guidelines:
- **Formats Accepted:** JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Maximum Size:** 10MB per image
- **Logo:** Square format works best (500x500px)
- **Banner:** Wide format (1200x400px or similar)

### Why We Need This:
Your store details help customers discover and recognize your brand.

### What You'll Need:
- ‚úÖ Bank Account Number

### Tips:
- **IFSC Code Format:** AAAA0XXXXXX (11 characters)
- Double-check your account number - typos can delay payouts
- Account holder name should **match your bank records**
- Use the account you want to receive payments

## Step 4: Product Setup (Optional)

### You Can:
- **Skip this step** and add products later from your dashboard
- Use our full Product Management system after approval

### If You Choose to Add Products Now:
You'll be redirected to the product creation interface where you can:
- Add product names, descriptions, prices
- Upload product images
- Set inventory quantities
- Configure variants (sizes, colors, etc.)

### Recommendation:
Most merchants prefer to **skip this step** and add products after their store is approved, when they can access the full dashboard features.

---

## Step 5: Document Verification (Required)

This is the **most important step**. We need to verify your business documents to ensure trust and safety on our platform.

### Required Documents (At Least 1):

#### 1. Business License
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Trade License
- Shop & Establishment License
- Any official business registration document

#### 2. ID Proof
- Aadhaar Card
- PAN Card
- Passport
- Driver's License
- Voter ID

#### 3. Address Proof
- Utility Bill (Electricity, Water, Gas)
- Bank Statement
- Rental Agreement
- Property Tax Receipt

#### 4. GST Certificate (If applicable)
- Official GST Registration Certificate
- Clear and readable copy

#### 5. PAN Card (If applicable)
- Individual or Business PAN Card
- Should match the PAN number provided in Step 1

### Document Requirements:
- **Format:** JPG, PNG, GIF, or PDF
- **Size:** Maximum 10MB per document
- **Quality:** Clear, readable, not blurred
- **Validity:** Current/not expired
- **Authenticity:** Original documents only

### Upload Tips:
1. **Scan or photograph** documents in good lighting
2. Ensure **all corners are visible**
3. Text should be **clearly readable**
4. No watermarks or edits
5. Upload **color copies** when possible

### How Many Documents?
- **Minimum:** 1 document
- **Recommended:** 3+ documents for faster verification
- More documents = faster approval!

## Verification Process

### What We Verify:
1. **Business Information** - Company name, registration, GST/PAN
2. **Document Authenticity** - Checking if documents are valid
3. **Address Confirmation** - Ensuring physical location exists
4. **Bank Account** - Verifying account ownership
5. **Identity** - Confirming business owner identity

### Common Reasons for Rejection:
- ‚ùå Blurry or unreadable documents
- ‚ùå Expired documents
- ‚ùå Mismatch between provided info and documents
- ‚ùå Invalid GST/PAN numbers
- ‚ùå Incorrect bank account details
- ‚ùå Incomplete information

### How to Avoid Rejection:
- ‚úÖ Provide **accurate information**
- ‚úÖ Upload **clear, high-quality** documents
- ‚úÖ Ensure **documents match** the information provided
- ‚úÖ Use **current/valid** documents
- ‚úÖ Fill **all required fields**

## FAQs

### Q: How long does verification take?
**A:** Typically 1-2 business days, sometimes faster if all documents are in order.

### Q: Can I edit my application after submission?
**A:** No, but if rejected, you can reapply with corrected information.

### Q: What if I don't have GST/PAN?
**A:** These are optional for most businesses. Provide them if available.

### Q: Can I upload documents later?
**A:** You must upload at least 1 document in Step 5 to complete onboarding.

### Q: What file formats are accepted?
**A:** JPG, PNG, GIF, and PDF files up to 10MB each.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes, we use bank-level encryption and security measures.

### Q: Can I change my bank details later?
**A:** Yes, you can update bank details from your dashboard after approval.

### Q: What if my documents are rejected?
**A:** We'll email you the specific reason. Address the issue and reupload.

### Q: Can I operate multiple stores?
**A:** Currently, one store per merchant account.

### Q: What happens to Step 4 (Products) if I skip it?
**A:** You can add products anytime from your dashboard after approval.

### Q: Can I pause and resume later?
**A:** Yes! Your progress is automatically saved. Resume anytime.

### Q: Who can I contact for help?
**A:** Email support@yourstore.com or use the chat feature.
